About Me

Hello! My name is Turner Jimerson Jr, I am a Javascript Developer. I started learning how to code with Javascript in 2020, attending the web design program at Eleven Fifty Academy. I am currently still attending, as a hopeful graduate of this program. Though I am new to this field, I have a strong desire to learn more and become the best developer I can be.

Since then I have always been in love with the process of brainstorming, creating, and improving on any project I'm working on, and I decided that this would be a pretty interesting field to get into because of that.

I am just 21 years old, with a lot of learning and growth ahead of me! You can feel free to check out any of my previous projects on Github! Also if you'd like, have a look at my resume.
